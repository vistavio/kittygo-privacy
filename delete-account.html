KittyGo – Delete Account & Data

You can permanently delete your KittyGo account and all associated data in two ways:

Option 1: Delete from within the app

1. Open KittyGo and go to your trip.
2. Tap the Settings icon (top right).
3. Scroll down and tap Delete Trip. Repeat for all trips.
4. All your trip data (plans, tips, expenses, photos) will be permanently deleted from our servers.

Option 2: Request deletion by email

Send an email to estevista1@gmail.com with the subject "KittyGo Account Deletion Request". Include the email address associated with your account. We will delete your account and all data within 7 days.

What gets deleted: All trip data, plans, tips, expenses, notes, and photos stored in our database. Your Google account itself is not affected.
